Section 04

Customer Base

Who are our customers, and how much are they worth?
Key Insight

63% of customers are one-timers (generating just 24% of revenue), while Power users (1% of base) drive 17% of revenue.

Key Insight

Converting just 10% of one-timers to Light users could add ~£581K in incremental revenue.

Key Insight

Churn varies significantly by store: Bond Street loses 77% of repeat customers, while Battersea Power Station retains best at 54%. High-churn locations may have an experience or service problem worth investigating on-site.

Key Insight

4,679 regular customers (6+ orders) are overdue relative to their own ordering pattern — not yet churned, but silent longer than usual. The 18% who use digital channels can be contacted; the rest can only be flagged if they return in-store.

Recommended Actions
  • For digital-channel customers (18%): automated second-order incentive within a week of their first visit.
  • For till-only customers (82%): prompt account sign-up at the till — this is the only way to enable follow-up contact.
  • Design a loyalty programme rewarding milestones (orders 3, 6, 10) to move Light users toward Regular.
