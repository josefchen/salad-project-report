Rating vs. Retention

Victoria is the network's highest-rated location at 4.9★, yet only 41% of customers return — strong food, but driven by transient footfall rather than habit. Bond Street scores 4.6★, ruling out quality as the cause of its high churn; the location's shopping/tourist catchment is the likely explanation. Bank, conversely, is the lowest-rated at 4.3★ but retains customers best (51% repeat rate) — a reminder that convenience and daily routine beat quality scores when it comes to building repeat business.

Recommended Actions
  • Investigate what high-AOV and high-retention stores do differently (menu mix, upselling, service speed, location demographics).
  • Transfer best practices from top performers to lower-ranked stores.
  • Review channel dependency — stores reliant on a single channel are vulnerable to platform changes.
  • Reallocate marketing budget toward highest-ROI locations.
