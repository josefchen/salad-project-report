Section 02b

What Customers Are Saying

Verified Google reviews across all locations — highlights, lowlights, and two operational flags that need attention now.
Key Finding

The food scores well across the network (4.3–4.9★ across 12 physical stores). But two operational issues surfaced in the past 4 weeks that need attention: food safety at Oxford Circus (undercooked chicken reported twice) and perceived portion shrinkage at Bank (recurring complaints since Jan 2026).

Cross-Store Insights
  • New stores outperforming: Canary Wharf, Battersea, Southwark, Victoria all scoring 4.5–4.9★ with highly positive recent reviews. Expansion quality is holding.
  • Staff excellence driving ratings: Victoria's "Jasmine" and Battersea's "Martina" both named individually by customers. Personal service is a key differentiator at top-rated stores.
  • Value perception: TCR and Bond Street reviewers use "value for money." Bank reviewers call £20 "a cash grab." Same product, different perception — likely portion-size-driven.
