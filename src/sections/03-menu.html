Section 03

Menu Intelligence

What sells, what's profitable, and where are the upsell opportunities?
Key Insight

"Build Your Own Salad" is the top seller, generating 24% of top-15 revenue.

Key Insight

Paid add-ons contribute 39% of customisable bundle revenue. 10% of orders skip add-ons entirely — significant upsell headroom.

Key Insight

"Chipotle caesar dressing" is the most popular dressing — feature it in marketing and default selections.

Recommended Actions
  • Prompt add-on suggestions at checkout for orders with zero paid add-ons — this alone could lift basket size materially.
  • Test bundle pricing to incentivise premium customisation (e.g. 'add avocado for £1').
  • Place high-margin items more prominently on menus and kiosk screens.
